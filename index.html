How to Order

Phase 1: Digital Submission

Submit your CAD files along with your project requirements. We conduct a preliminary DFM review to ensure alignment between your design intent and production capabilities.

Phase 2: Commercial Quotation

Receive a comprehensive cost estimate, including material selection, machining time, and logicstics. At this stage, we finalize the strategy and consensus before moving to fabrication.

Phase 3: Precision Fabrication

Once your order is placed is completed, your product will enter the manufacturing process.

Phase 4: Rigorous Quality Assurance

We implement strict metrological inspection protocols. Every component is verified against your specified tolerances before final approval.

Phase 5: Fulfillment & Delivery

Delivery will be arranged based on the logistics option selected by the customer and shipped directly to the designated address.
